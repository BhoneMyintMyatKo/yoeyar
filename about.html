About Us

The purpose of the "Yoe Yar" website is to create an engaging and user-friendly e-commerce platform that showcases and sells authentic Burmese traditional products. It aims to reflect the cultural richness of Burma and highlight the uniqueness of these traditional products.

History

Yoe Yar Comapy is a distinguished company that curates and presents an exclusive collection of authentic Burmese Traditional Products.

Mission

The targeted audience for the "Yoe Yar" website includes individuals who have an interest in Burmese traditional arts and culture, as well as those who appreciate authentic and unique products. The website aims to attract both local and international customers who are looking to purchase high-quality traditional products from Burma.
